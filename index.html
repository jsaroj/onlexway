Legal Practice Summary

With more than 5 years of experience, specializes in various legal domains:

Intellectual Property (Trademark)
  • Conducted trademark searches and classified goods/services under the Nice Classification.
  • Filed trademark applications and handled oppositions and cancellation proceedings.
  • Issued Cease and Desist notices and managed trademark renewals and maintenance.
Insolvency & Bankruptcy
  • Managed IBC matters and represented clients before the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT).
  • Drafted petitions, demand notices, and defense arguments for creditors.
  • Attended CIRP and CoC meetings, drafting minutes and conducting legal research.
Civil & Commercial Litigation
  • Drafted pleadings, written statements, and counterclaims for civil matters.
  • Handled breach of contract cases and real estate injunction petitions.
  • Managed cases under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act.
Corporate Law & Compliance
  • Assisted in company incorporations and handled registrations and filings.
  • Managed annual compliances and MCA-related compliance matters.
  • Drafted and reviewed corporate filings and handled legal strategies.
Commercial Drafting
  • Drafted contracts such as NDAs, Service Agreements, and MOUs.
  • Reviewed Shareholder and Joint Venture Agreements.
  • Negotiated and drafted Supply and Purchase Agreements.
Family Law
  • Managed mutual consent divorce proceedings and advised clients on legal strategies.
  • Drafted settlement agreements and mediated discussions between parties.
  • Ensured compliance with legal formalities and court orders.
